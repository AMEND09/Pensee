Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Overview

Pensée ("we", "our", or "the app") is a daily writing tool. We believe your writing and personal data are yours. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how it is protected.

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for authentication and account management.
  • Display name — shown in the app and used to personalise your experience.
  • Writing sessions — your timed writing entries, associated terms used, reflection notes, and word counts.
  • OAuth tokens — if you sign in with Google, we receive a token scoped only to your basic profile (email and name). We do not access your Google Drive, Gmail, or any other Google data.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To store and sync your writing sessions across devices.
  • To display your personal writing history and statistics within the app.
  • To improve the app based on usage patterns (non-personally-identifiable aggregate data only).

Data Storage

Your data is stored on a PocketBase server. It is not shared with any third party beyond what is required to provide the core service (authentication via Google OAuth).

Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all writing sessions from within the app.
  • Deletion: You may delete your account at any time from the Account screen. This permanently removes all associated data from our servers.
  • Correction: You may update your display name at any time from the Account screen.

Children's Privacy

Pensée is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent version.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us via the GitHub repository or the contact information listed on the App Store listing.
