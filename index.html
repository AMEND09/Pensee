What is a Pensée?

1. Literary
A thought or reflection put into written form; a fragment of an idea captured before it escapes.
2. Botanical
A pansy — the flower of remembrance, whose name in French means "thought."

Your mind is a garden. Pensée gives you a single seed each day — an evocative quote — along with three rhetorical or vocabulary tools to guide your hand. Ten minutes to tend to what grows.

I

The Seed

Writer's block is decision paralysis. We remove it. Each day you receive one carefully chosen quote — your anchor and your invitation.

II

The Technique

Three rhetorical devices or vocabulary terms accompany every prompt. They aren't rules — they're pathways into language you haven't tried yet.

III

The Ten Minutes

A timer creates urgency. You aren't writing a novel — you're capturing a thought. When it ends, you reflect and save. Growth is incremental.
